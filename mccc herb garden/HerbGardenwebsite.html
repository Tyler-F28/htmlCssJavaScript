Who We Are

Rooted in Medina County since 1976.

The Medina County Herb Society is a welcoming group of volunteers who share a passion for gardening. We are a non-profit organization focused on sharing knowledge about the history, use, and cultivation of herbs.

  • Community outreach and educational programs.

  • Public herb gardens and local projects.

  • Volunteer opportunities for all skill levels.

"Sharing the love of herbs with our community."

Herb of the Season: Purple Basil

A Royal Addition to the Garden

Purple basil isn't just beautiful; it's packed with flavor. Use it to make a stunning violet vinegar or add a pop of color to summer salads.
