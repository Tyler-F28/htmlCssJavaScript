About Us

Cultivating knowledge and friendship in the garden.

The MCCC Herb Society is a student and community organization dedicated to the education, cultivation, and enjoyment of herbs. Whether you're a master gardener or just bought your first basil plant, there's a place for you here.

  • Maintain the campus medicinal herb garden.

  • Host seasonal workshops on planting, harvesting, and usage.

  • Promote sustainability and organic gardening practices.

Herb of the Month: Rosemary

Rosmarinus officinalis

The Dew of the Sea

Rosemary is a woody, perennial herb with fragrant, evergreen, needle-like leaves and white, pink, purple, or blue flowers, native to the Mediterranean region.
